FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions. Still stuck? Hop into Discord.
Is ChillOutMods free to use?
Yes — the launcher and the entire mod library are free. You only need ChilloutVR installed (also free on Steam).
Will mods break with new ChilloutVR updates?
Our launcher auto-updates mods within hours of new ChilloutVR releases. If something breaks, our community usually patches it before the dev team finishes their coffee.
Are these mods safe? Will I get banned?
Every mod is reviewed and signed. We only host client-side cosmetic and quality-of-life mods — nothing that violates ChilloutVR's terms of service.
Can I run multiple mods at once?
Absolutely. The launcher's profile system lets you mix and match, and warns you about conflicts before you launch.
What are the system requirements?
Anything that runs ChilloutVR will run our mods. For visual mods (4K textures, ray tracing), an RTX 3060 or better is recommended.
How do I submit my own mod?
Join our Discord and ping the #creator-portal channel. We review submissions weekly and help with packaging if needed.
Does the launcher collect any data?
Only anonymous crash reports and version metrics so we can ship fixes faster. Never personal info, never your login. You can opt out in settings.
Can I use mods offline?
Yes. Once a mod is installed, it works offline. The launcher only needs internet to fetch updates and download new mods.
